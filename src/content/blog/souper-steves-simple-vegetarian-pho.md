A simple vegetarian pho that is quick to make and easy to customize.

## Ingredients

### Noodles
- 1 handful rice noodles

### Broth
- 1 box vegetarian pho broth

### Veggies
- broccoli
- carrot
- onion
- green onion
- optional Thai basil
- optional lime

### Tofu
- 1 block tofu, cubed

### Tofu marinade
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 1/4 tsp garlic powder
- 1/4 tsp onion powder
- optional 1 tbsp maple syrup

## Instructions

1. Marinate tofu for 5-10 minutes and air-fry at 390°F for about 15 minutes.
2. Cook rice noodles according to package instructions.
3. Heat broth and add veggies in stages (carrots, then onions, then broccoli).
4. Assemble noodles + broth + veggies + tofu.
5. Garnish with green onion, basil, and lime.
